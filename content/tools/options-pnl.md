Prices options using the Black-Scholes model and visualizes P&L outcomes across different stock prices and volatility levels. Build multi-leg strategies and analyze payoff diagrams, heatmaps, and Greek exposures to understand your risk/reward profile.

Define legs (calls and puts) with strike prices and quantities. The calculator plots your profit/loss surface and shows how the strategy behaves as the underlying stock moves and volatility changes.
